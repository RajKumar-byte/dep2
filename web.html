Connecting Surplus Food with Those Who Need It

FoodShare bridges the gap between restaurants with surplus food and NGOs helping those in need. Together, we can reduce food waste and fight hunger.

Key Features

Our platform makes food donation simple, efficient, and impactful

Custom Dashboards

Tailored interfaces for restaurants and NGOs to manage donations efficiently

Real-time Notifications

Instant alerts when food is available or pickups are scheduled

Interactive Map

Easily locate nearby restaurants with surplus food available

Food Safety Guidelines

Best practices to ensure safe food handling and donation

Impact Tracker

Measure your contribution to reducing waste and helping communities

Smart Matching

Automated system connects restaurants with nearby NGOs based on needs
