Benefits of Joining FoodShare Connect

  • For Restaurants: Reduce food waste, receive tax benefits for donations, enhance corporate social responsibility, and connect with your community.
  • For NGOs: Access consistent food donations, expand your organization's reach, and increase the variety of food available to those you serve.
  • For Communities: Decrease food waste, support sustainable practices, and ensure food is distributed to those who need it most.
